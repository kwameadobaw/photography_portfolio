Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book a photography session?

For weddings and major events, we recommend booking 6-12 months in advance. For portrait sessions, 2-4 weeks notice is typically sufficient, but availability may vary during peak seasons.

What happens if it rains on the day of an outdoor shoot?

We monitor weather forecasts closely and will contact you 24-48 hours before your session if weather appears to be an issue. We can either reschedule or move to an indoor location, depending on your preference.

How long until I receive my photos?

You'll receive a preview of select images within 1 week of your session. The complete gallery will be delivered within 2-3 weeks for portrait sessions and 4-6 weeks for weddings and events.

Do you travel for photography sessions?

Yes! We love to travel for photography sessions. Travel fees may apply depending on the location. Please contact us for a custom quote for destination photography.
