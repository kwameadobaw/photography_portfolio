Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I book?

After submitting your booking request, you'll receive a confirmation email within 24 hours. We'll then schedule a consultation call to discuss your vision and plan the details of your session.

What if I need to reschedule?

We understand that things come up. You can reschedule your session up to 48 hours before your appointment without any fee. Cancellations within 48 hours may be subject to a rebooking fee.

How long until I receive my photos?

You'll receive a preview of your images within 1 week of your session. The complete gallery will be delivered within 2-3 weeks, depending on the package selected.

Do you offer custom packages?

Yes! If you have specific needs that aren't covered by our standard packages, please contact us to create a custom package tailored to your requirements.
